    > Anonymous exports are now cleaned up automatically after they expire. A
    > new recurring background task deletes export files and their
    > corresponding records once they are older than the value defined in
    > `ANON_EXPORTS_CLEANUP_AGE` (configured in Constance, default: 30
    > minutes).
    > 
    > To ensure system stability, the cleanup runs in small batches of 50
    > exports per execution and uses a distributed lock to prevent multiple
    > workers from deleting the same exports simultaneously. This keeps
    > storage usage under control and prevents outdated anonymous export files
    > from remaining accessible.
    > 
    > Authenticated exports and other project data are not affected.
